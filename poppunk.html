A Digital Pop Punk Museum

According to Wikipedia, pop punk is "a rock music genre that combines the textures and fast tempos of punk rock
with the melodies and chord progressions of power pop and pop music." Over the years, pop punk's mainstream
success has fluctuated, with a recent resurgence that some attribute to the social media platform TikTok. Below, you
can find the major eras of the genre as well as some key artists for each. Each page includes links to popular songs
that are good starting points for your exploration of the genre.

Origins: 1970s-1980s

Rise to Mainstream: 1994-2004

Emo Pop and Neon Pop-Punk: 2005-2009

Maturation and Decline in Popularity: 2010s

Mainstream Resurgence: 2020s
